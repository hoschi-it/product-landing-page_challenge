Who are rubber ducks?

Well, this question is not that simple to answer. Some of them are nice, some don't like company. Some are silly, some are grown up or just boring.

Though people tend to judge them, there is not much they have in common:

  • Most of them have yellow coat.
  • They have a beak.
  • They hush, unless they are put under pressure.
  • They can swim on water.
  • They are very patient.

So what is all this "every rubber duck is the same"? It is simply not true.
